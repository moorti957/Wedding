FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in your wedding photography packages?
Our packages include professional photography coverage, edited high-resolution images, online gallery access, and a personalized experience. Gold and Platinum packages add videography, drone coverage, and premium albums.
How far in advance should we book?
We recommend booking 6–12 months in advance to secure your date, especially during peak wedding season. Contact us for availability.
Do you travel for destination weddings?
Absolutely! We love capturing love stories around the world. Destination weddings are our specialty, and we offer custom packages for travel.
How long does it take to receive the final photos?
We deliver a sneak peek within 48 hours, and the full gallery is typically ready within 4–6 weeks. Videography may take 6–8 weeks for the final cinematic film.
